Hi, I'm Ori! I'm a graduate student at the University of Tübingen and the International Max Planck Research School for Intelligent Systems (IMPRS-IS), working in Matthias Bethge's lab. I'm interested in closing the gap between how machine learning models perform in known benchmarks versus their performance in more complicated real-world scenarios.

During my PhD, I had the privilege of collaborating with Ravid Shwartz-Ziv, Yann LeCun, and Ofir Press.

Previously, I graduated from Tel Aviv University with a BSc in Mathematics and an MSc in Computer Science, advised by Lior Wolf.

My brother Ofir Press is a machine learning researcher.
